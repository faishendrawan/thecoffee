About Us

Why choose our coffee?

The coffee is a coffee company that dedicates all of our passion, love and enthusiasm to the amazing world of coffee. We are a local company and most of the raw materials we use are local.

We know, and so do you, that the world's best coffee beans come from Indonesia. We travel to various parts of Indonesia and work closely with local coffee farmers and roasters to get the best Indonesian coffee flavors, first grade Arabica coffee beans and various plantations throughout the archipelago.

With our experience and knowledge in the retail coffee industry, from the processing of coffee beans to how to design a coffee bar, we make your coffee business journey EASY, SIMPLE, and FUN!
